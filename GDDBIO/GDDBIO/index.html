Bio

I am a Game Design & Development major at Quinnipiac university. I would like to focus on the look and feel in video games, I find that when playing some of my favorite Games I'm always looking for improvements that can be made to further the immersive experience. Overall, My goal is to gain the position of a lead designer guiding a team through its roadblocks and keeping a game's essence intact and making sure the product invokes the proper response from its audience.
